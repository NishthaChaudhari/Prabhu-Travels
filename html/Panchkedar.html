PANCHKEDAR
Panch Kedar is located in Kedarkhand of Garhwal division of Uttarakhand. First KEDARNATH second MADHYMHESHWAR, third TUNGNATH, fourth RUDRANATH, fifth KALPESHWAR come in Panch Kedar. There are mythological temples dedicated to Lord Shiva in these five places. The description of Panch Kedar is given in Sakanda Purana. It is described in the Mahabharata also in PanchKedar is mentioned.
PANCHKEDAR KATHA
According to the Puranas, after the Mahabharata war, the Pandavas were accused of fratricide. To get rid of this sin, the Pandavas thought of going to Mahadev, the God of Gods, to seek his blessings. But Shiva did not want to give darshan to the Pandavas... Shiva got angry and hid in Kedarkhand... When the Pandavas could not see Shiva even after reaching Kedarkhand.. then the Pandavas started searching for Shiva. When Mahadev felt that the Pandavas would find him.. then Shiva took the form of a bull.. started walking among the animals. Bhima got suspicious. Whether or not Shankar is hidden among these animals.. Bhima took a huge form and stepped on two opposite rocks and the rest of the Pandavas started driving those animals under Bhima's feet. All the cows and bulls got scared and ran away from under Bhima's feet.. But Bhima, the bull in the form of Shiva. Started running in the opposite direction from Bhim's feet.. Bhim understood that this is Bholenath..Mahabali Bhima ran and grabbed the hump of the bull. When Bhima started controlling the bull in the form of Shiva.. Shiva started to merge inside the earth.. But even then Bhima did not leave the back of the bull. Then Mahadev stopped this stubborn devotion of Pandavas Devoted to yoga and pleased, appeared to the Pandavas and freed them from sin. Shiva's back part appeared in Kedarnath, middle part navel in Madhyamaheshwar, arms in Tungnath, face in Rudranath, Jata appeared in Kalpeshwar.. Front part appeared in Pashupatinath Nepal.. Later Pandavas built temples at these places got built.
